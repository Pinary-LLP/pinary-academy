Is this coding course suitable for complete beginners?

Yes. Pinary is designed for students and beginners who need a structured path, mentor support, and weekly review instead of random tutorials.

Will I learn AI tools for development?

Yes. You will learn how to use AI tools for planning, debugging, documentation, and faster development while still building strong fundamentals.

How does weekly review work?

You submit work every week. Mentors review code quality, structure, clarity, and communication so you can fix mistakes and improve standards.

Does Pinary give a job guarantee?

No fake job guarantee is promised. Pinary focuses on job-oriented training, portfolio-ready work, review habits, communication, and career confidence.

Will this help me with freelance work?

Yes. You will learn how to understand client requirements, give quotations, communicate professionally, close basic projects, and deliver work properly.
